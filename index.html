Route Through These Cities (optional)

Type a city name you want the route to go through. This is how you avoid roads — pick a city far enough away from the road you want to avoid that the trip is forced to detour through it.

Example: To avoid I-70 east of Topeka on a Hays → Indianapolis trip, type "Wichita, KS" below. That forces the route south of I-70.
Don't type road names like "I-70" — only city names work.
